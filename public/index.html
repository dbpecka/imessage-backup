How it works

Three steps.
Zero surprises.

Bubble Wrap walks you through the whole process one screen at a time. Nothing happens until you say so.

01
Choose what to back up

Pick a date range, select the conversations you care about, and choose a folder on your Mac to save everything in.

02
Back up your messages

Bubble Wrap exports your conversations and copies all attachments to your folder. A live progress bar and full summary keep you informed the whole way.

03
Optionally clean up

Once your backup is complete, you can remove old messages and attachments from your Mac's database to free up space. This step is entirely optional — skip it if you want.
